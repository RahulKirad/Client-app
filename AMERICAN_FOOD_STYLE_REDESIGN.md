## Overview
Your Cottoniq website has been completely transformed with a vibrant green food-style aesthetic matching the template design, while preserving all existing content and functionality.

## Design Changes Applied

### Color Palette (Matching Template Design)
- **Olive/Lime Green**: `#6B8E23` - Primary brand color
- **White**: `#FFFFFF` - Clean backgrounds
- **Dark Charcoal**: `#2C3E50` - Headings and primary text
- **Gray**: `#5A6C7D` - Body text and secondary content
- **Light Gray**: `#F5F5F5` / `#F9FAFB` - Section backgrounds

### Typography & Visual Style
- **Clean, modern typography** - Bold headings without excessive styling
- **Rounded corners** - Buttons use `rounded-full`, cards use `rounded-xl`
- **Soft shadows** - Modern shadow effects (e.g., `shadow-lg`, `shadow-xl`)
- **Minimal borders** - Subtle 1-2px borders where needed
- **Clean, professional layout** - Food-inspired but modern aesthetic
